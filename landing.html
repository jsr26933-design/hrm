❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does implementation take?
Most companies are fully set up within 1–3 days. Our guided onboarding wizard walks you through company setup, employee import, and policy configuration step by step.
Can I import existing employee data?
Yes. You can bulk import employees via Excel or CSV. We provide ready-made templates and our team assists with data migration at no extra cost.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. WorkPilot HRM uses 256-bit AES encryption, daily backups, ISO 27001-compliant infrastructure, role-based access control, and audit logs for all admin actions.
Does it work for field workforce?
Yes — our GPS attendance with geo-fencing and live tracking map is specifically designed for field sales, delivery agents, site workers, and remote employees.
What statutory compliance is covered?
We handle PF (12% employer + employee), ESI, Professional Tax (all states), TDS computation, Form 16 generation, and all statutory reports required for Indian payroll compliance.
Can I manage multiple companies?
Yes. The Growth plan supports up to 3 companies and Enterprise supports unlimited companies, all manageable from a single login with separate data for each entity.
Is there a mobile app?
WorkPilot HRM is fully responsive and works on all devices. A dedicated Android & iOS app is available for employee self-service, GPS attendance, and manager approvals.
What kind of support do you offer?
Starter plans include email support with 24-hour response. Growth plans have priority support with 4-hour response. Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager and phone support.
