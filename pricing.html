Pricing FAQ

How is pricing calculated?
Starter plan is a flat annual fee. Growth and Enterprise are per-employee per-month, billed monthly or annually. You only pay for active employees.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get access immediately. Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle.
Is there really no credit card for the trial?
Correct. Start your 14-day free trial with just your email. No payment info required until you decide to upgrade.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You can export all your data before cancellation. After 90 days of account closure, data is permanently deleted per our retention policy.
